Things to think about while watching silent film

  • Camera work
    • mostly static camera work
    • experience-based and technology-based (no lenses yet)
    • long shot (establishing shot): wide angle shot
    • medium shot: full body to torso
    • medium close shot
    • close up: face
    • extreme close up: individual facial features
  • Acting
    • super exaggerated from theater
  • Lighting
    • early film makers started in Jersey, but then moved to California
    • so much sun, an make movies all year round
  • Editing
    • basic cuts, etc
  • Special effects
    • explosions, color, etc.
  • How female characters are portrayed
    • Lonedale operator: clearly an active participator in the story
    • this story: drives action
    • need to be saved; start sweeping when they're idle

Buster Keaton (Heroes of the Day) vs. Early films

  • Camera work
    • moves: pans, long shots
  • Acting
    • not theatrical. get a sense of what they're doing and feeling.
  • Editing
    • Lots of well-made cuts
    • multiple simultaneous locations
    • title cards
